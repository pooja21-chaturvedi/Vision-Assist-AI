ℹ️ About Vision Assist AI
Our Mission
Vision Assist AI is designed to empower visually challenged users with cutting-edge AI technology. We combine Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Generative AI to create an accessible digital experience that puts independence and empowerment at the forefront.
Key Features
• Voice-controlled navigation: Navigate through all features using natural speech
• Multi-language support: Available in English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali
• Real-time scene recognition: AI-powered computer vision for environment understanding
• Document reading: Intelligent text-to-speech for documents and emails
• Indian currency detection: Specialized recognition for Indian banknotes
• Smart text editing: Voice-controlled document creation and editing
• System integration: Direct control of computer applications and functions
Accessibility Standards
This application is built with accessibility as the primary focus, featuring high contrast design, keyboard navigation support, screen reader compatibility, and comprehensive voice control. All interactions provide audio feedback and visual confirmation.
